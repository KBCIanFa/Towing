🇦🇺 Australian Towing Law Notes
• Trailer brakes required when GTM exceeds 750 kg in all states
• Electric brakes typically required when ATM exceeds 2,000 kg (varies by state)
• Ball weight must not exceed manufacturer's max towball mass
• Vehicle must not exceed GVM when ball weight is added
• Combined vehicle + trailer must not exceed GCM
• WA, QLD, SA, VIC may have state-specific variations — check your state RMS/TMR
