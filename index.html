Why is there no free AU towing API?
Towing capacity, GVM and GCM are manufacturer-published values specific to each vehicle variant — they are not standardised in any government open-data registry. Paid services like InfoAgent AU and Blue Flag aggregate this data at a cost. For this app, AU towing specs are sourced directly from manufacturer documentation and overlaid on top of live API data where a make/model match is found.
